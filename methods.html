The Craft of Kombucha

How We Cultivate Life

Our Kombucha is not just fermented tea; it is a meticulously controlled biological process. We follow a specific sequence of stages to ensure the perfect balance of organic acids, probiotics, and refined flavor.

01

The Infusion

Everything starts with premium organic tea and filtered water. As a Sous-Chef, I select the blend to ensure a sophisticated base, rich in tannins that will feed the culture and define the body of the elixir.

02

Symbiotic Growth

The "SCOBY" (Symbiotic Culture of Bacteria and Yeast) is introduced. In this phase, technical precision is key: we monitor temperature and pH levels daily to achieve the optimal probiotic concentration without over-acidification.

03

Natural Conditioning

The final touch. After the first fermentation, the Kombucha is bottled for a secondary natural carbonation. No artificial CO2 is added; the fine, champagne-like bubbles are the result of pure, living biology.
